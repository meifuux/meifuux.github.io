Who am I?

As a creative from Hong Kong, I have brought my skills and experience to Toronto, Canada, where I am currently seeking opportunities in UX/UI design. With over two years of experience in this field, I have developed a passion for ideation, storytelling, and visual design. In my free time, I enjoy exploring my creativity by making craft dolls, coding interactive Arduino with 3D printing. While amazed with all aspect of User Experience design, I find myself interested the most in understanding the motivations and behaviours of users, and I am always striving to create experiences that are both effective and pretty user interface.
