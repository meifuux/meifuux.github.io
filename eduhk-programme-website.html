Define & Ideation 

Design Background

This project is introduced to promote for a new programme of Department of Curriculum and Instruction of EduHK. I had start off the project with the programme leader regarding what is the aims, target and information shall be included. It's a simple, low budget and short production time project.

  • Target audience : Student who are interested in register a minor programme.
  • Aims : Promotion of new programme & information providing website.
  • Production timeline : Within 1.5 month

Design Requirement

After conversation, the programmer leader wish to make the site easy to navigation. She wish to keep things clear & simple. Also to be reminded fit the branding scheme be-hide.

Based on this, I had bear 3-click rule in mind during the development progress.

Draft idea

Based on this, I had briefly drafted a design for programme leader to preview and make comments. The design is created to click on fixed location navigation bar and scroll down to the content.

After receiving feedback, programmer leader don't hope to have much animation (i.e. scrolling animation effect). She thinks it's dazzled and wish to improve on this part. However, she like the back to top button and fixed navigation.

Design Guide

Based on the comments, I had further created a design guide for later development usage. Keeps all the design in consistency.

Learnings

Communication with Clients

The whole project was for internal usage, however there are many limits and requirements for the whole design. Communications is the most important skills through out the whole progress. To discussion and understand the needs and idea from the customers so you could full-fill their needs and make suggests for better design / performance. Sometimes, even client not sure what they are looking for. I had to frequently ask and guide to see what they are looking for. That's why communication skill is a key element for a successful project.
