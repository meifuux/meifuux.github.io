Design outcome

Clear & Understandable

Restructure architecture & rename features

Through rename schemes & restructure the architecture, to resolve the messiness of the original Birdie Mobile design. Let users to click on features they wanted in limited clicks.

Convince & all in one

Introduce family plans and all in one feature

"Family" is one of the biggest target users for Birdie Mobile. All-in-one display and payment feature could help family plan users finish tasks quickly.
"Family plan" could become an inducement in term of marketing with discount included as well.

Never forget importants

Pop-up notification & clear interface notification

You will never gonna miss out any discount / billing / event anymore. We have got pop-up notification and clear UI to remind you what you haven't read.

Research

Survey & Comments

To start off, I had created a survey in who and how the users think of the Birdie Mobile app. Moreover, conduct a semi-structured interview with some Birdie Mobile users.

Some of the main findings are : 

Users’ age are spread. 2 main age groups are noticed, the teenagers to young adults. While some of them the whole family had purchase plans from Mobile Birdie. And some are introduced Birdie Mobile though friends.

They choose to use Mobile Birdie as it’s avoid complex contract (freedom), having P2P transfer feature and cheap.

They like the idea they can have free data by playing the Mobile Birdie Farm app.

User’s interested in "Birdie Friday" offer grab events. However, they always miss it as there’s no notifications.

They need to dig in and spend time to find some of the features. Not in 1st sight.

User workflow

I had also invite some users to preform several tasks with time recording, aims to find out which part having most serious problem with.

  • Task 1 : Check how many data and when is due date.
  • Task 2 : Check any discount provided this Friday.
  • Task 3 : P2P 0.5BG to me (Phone mobile provided)
  • Task 4 : Look for other provided plans from Birdie
  • Task 5 : Purchase for more data
An error occurred in Task2, a participate never notice this feature. He spend time browser through all the bottom navgaionat however still not finding what he need. When review why he couldn’t find it. He reply the naming scheme is confusing and the related item are not under tab. So he dig-in all the pages he think it might be.
‍
2 participates had similar feedback for Task 4, not having a clear tag / couldn’t find where to look for. Most of the time spend in browsing page to page.

Competitive analysis

For dig into deeper with a different perspective, I conduct a competitive analysis of existing telecommunications provider platforms in Hong Kong market. To understand more about their strength and weakness.

More details

After analysis, it’s found that :

  • Convince & fast are the key requirement for these apps. E-payment method to remote ticket are introduced to them.
  • Users also care about time-saving and security. They like the idea for FaceID while hate one-time password.
  • Key information in 1st sight is important for users.

Pain points & solutions

01

Problem 1 - Messy architecture & Naming scheme

Users need to browser through page by page to look for what they really needs.

Solution 1 - Rename and recreate a better information architecture.

Rename the scheme into understandable terms. Restructure to group related items together. To improve a better navigation for users.

02

Problem 2 - Missing notification

Users always miss-out “Birdie Friday” events / other discount expire day.

Solution - Create notification

Pop-up notification shall be provided to due with the problem. By no means, different type of notification could be included : Pop-up, email, SMS etc.

03

Problem 3 - Not having easy monitor

From the research, it's noticed that a large percentage are using Birdie with their family. Certain improvement will help them in purchase plans, sharing & bill paying.

Solution - Family plan

Family plan could be introduced for purchase/ discount etc. In marketing p.o.v. could attract more family based users to use Birdie Mobile as a group.

Final design solution

01

Problem 1 - Messy architecture & Naming scheme

The bottom navigation didn't listed most frequently used features.
Naming scheme of the items are unclear. Users don't know what is the feature of the categories.

Solution - Architecture restructure & Feature rename

Rename the all feature's title to more easy understandable titles.
Most frequently use feature listed in bottom navigation bar, which users can switch quickly and easily.

02

Problem 2 - Missing notification

Users always miss-out “Birdie Friday” events / other discount expire day.

Solution - Create notification

Pop-up notification is introduced for all kinds of event, activity, due dates.
A specific tab is created for "Birdie Friday" offers. A fixed location could help users to locate event page.
Unread notifications (Red bullet) will appear if users haven't review the notification.

03

Problem 3 - Not having monitor

From the research, it's noticed that a large percentage are using Birdie with their family. Certain improvement will help them in purchase plans, sharing & bill paying.

Solution - Family plan

Family plan could be introduced for purchase/ discount etc. In marketing p.o.v. could attract more family based users to use Birdie Mobile as a group.
Monitor, P2P data transfer with recent usage and family plan bill can let user to action for whole family in one-click.

Evaluate

Usability Testing

For evaluate of the design, usability test is conducted with Birdie Mobile users. After introduce the project and some demographic questions (age range, level for experience in claw gaming, etc.) The following tasks are asked again to completed by the testers.

  • Task 1 : Check how many data and when is due date.
  • Task 2 : Check any discount provided this Friday.
  • Task 3 : P2P 0.5BG to me (Phone mobile provided)
  • Task 4 : Look for other provided plans from Birdie
  • Task 5 : Purchase for more data

I chat with them about the overall experience using the app, how effective they think of the solution, what is the best part and parts need to improve. The like the solution cause :

  • The bottom navigation is in better instructions compare to original. With all features they used most frequently.
  • They like pop-up promotion notification. They won’t miss any updates, like discount. It's helpful for them.
  • Family plan is convenient for payment and monitor in on-click.
  • User-friendly with the recent send users when using P2P data transfer.
