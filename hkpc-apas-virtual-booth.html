Design Background

This project is introduced to create virtual booth including webpage workflow, UI design as well as the mini-games' graphic. The booth will greatly introduce what HKPC & APAS are. Our team is working to create all the interaction in virtual environment in respond to government's COVID-19 action.

  • Aims : Introduce HKPC & APAS, via interactive gaming and display broad in virtual booth.
  • Production timeline : Within 3 weeks

Design Requirement

Client side wish to create a booth to help visitors better understand their department. Clear and simple is what client -side looking for. Also, time limited is fixed as they have to meet the exhibition starting date.
