Gov. ABO website reform & e-platform

The ABO, under the HKSAR's Leisure and Cultural Services Department, request to revamp their website and create a new e-platform for teacher. This project catering to three groups: the public, ABO officers, and teachers. Simplify the layout and ensure user-friendliness for all is our design goal for the project.
