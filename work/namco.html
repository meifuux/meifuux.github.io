Namco
Claw Gaming App

Claw gaming has become mainstream activity in recent years. Despite its popularity, the user experience for many claw gamers is still suboptimal. App interface ‘Namco’ aims to introduce a range of features that improve the gameplay experience for claw gamers. Whether you are a seasoned claw gamer or new to the scene, app ‘Namco’ is sure to provide a more immersive and rewarding experience.
DELIVERABLE
Crane-Game Assist Application with IoT control of the machines
SETTING
Solo Project
TIMELINE
6 weeks

Problem

How might we improve gameplay satisfaction for claw machine players when maintaining the needs of the business?

My Solution

Imaging a future Namco adopt an interface enhance the overall enjoyment, convenience,  as well as make it easier for players to track and connect with other players.

Professional Assistance

Scan the QR code for range of professional tips and assistance on your specific machine.We'll guide you through any issues you may encounter and provide assistance whenever you need it.

Community Rating System

Using the community rating system, you can choose what you want to play. You can filter the options by machine type and difficulty. Our system can help you narrow down the options in your mind.

Member Perks

Earn more discounts the more you play! As a member, you'll have access to rewards. We'll remind you when they expire, so you can make the most of them.

Smart Recharge

Use our online e-payment options to quickly and easily recharge your member card. Avoid long lines and get back to gameplay faster.

Bathroom Break

Need to use the bathroom during gameplay? We allow players to pause the machine for a short time. You don't need to worry about others taking your winnings.

My Process

Product Background

BANDAI NAMCO

Expert Provider of All Ages Entrainment

Product Position

Namco Limited was a video game company known for producing high-quality arcade and home console games. Its products were positioned as fun and entertaining. In addition to its video game offerings, the company also produced amusement park attractions and operated video arcades. This project will be focusing arcades.

User Insight & Research

I began the project by interviewing claw machine players, who are the target audience. Initially, I was unsure if my predictions were accurate, so I used these interviews to gather key insights to help inform my understanding of the audience and their needs.

Competitive Analysis

I analyzed the user flow of a claw game player in order to identify the pain points. Additionally, I conducted a competitive analysis of other claw machine game platforms to understand how they are outperforming Namco.

Research Conclusions

Based off the different interviews conducted and researches, I've narrowed down our insights into 3 user needs that wanted to address -- The design goal
from there, I created the persona, user journey, and eventually developed out the final product, Namco App interface.

Wireframe & Testing

I presented the prototype for players to provide feedback on. During the testing process, I’ve asked users to think aloud and share their thoughts. After the play-through was complete, we asked them to rate the effectiveness of the solution.

Usability Test Details

keyboard_arrow_down
To evaluate the effectiveness of the design, a usability test was conducted with claw game players. The test included an introduction to the project and some demographic questions (age range, level of experience in claw gaming). The participants were asked to complete the following tasks:
  • Task 1: Select the beginner level machine and review the tips video.
  • Task 2: Recharge your membership and select Mastercard as the payment method.
  • Task 3: Hold the specific machine from Task 1 for 5 seconds and then release it.
  • Task 4: Share your thoughts on the machine with the community.
The results of the test were quantified using the System Usability Scale (SUS) :
The design was well-received by users, with an above-average SUS score of 82.95. They appreciated the helpful tips, ease of use, convenience of e-payments and machine holding, and discount storage feature.
